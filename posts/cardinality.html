Types are sets of values

The following two statements are equivalent:
var x: u8
x$ \in u8$ where $ u8 \equiv \{ 0,1, ... 255\}$

Cardinality of sets types

Since we now recognize that u8 is a set we can take its cardinality. $|u8| = 256$ With good reflection, its possible to take the cardinality of any type.
